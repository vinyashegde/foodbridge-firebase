HISTORY

The project's history began with the recognition of the significant amount of food wasted daily in catering services, hotels, and other events. The founders of the project noticed that this wasted food could be repurposed to help alleviate hunger in the community. With this idea in mind, they began to research and develop a plan to collect and distribute the excess food to those in need.

MISSION

The mission of the project is to reduce food waste while also addressing the issue of food insecurity. The project seeks to accomplish this by collecting unused food from catering services, hotels, and other events and redistributing it to those in need. By doing so, the project hopes to make a positive impact on the community and help address the issue of hunger.

VISION

The vision of the project is to create a sustainable solution to food waste and hunger. The project aims to create a network of volunteers who are passionate about addressing food waste and hunger. Additionally, the project aims to utilise the food waste to make chemical-free manure for agriculture, further promoting sustainable and environmentally friendly practices.





Know More About Us!

Many people living in poverty struggle to afford or access healthy and nutritious food, leading to malnourishment and health issues. Large amounts of food are often wasted in catering services, hotels, and events, which could otherwise be repurposed to help those in need.
